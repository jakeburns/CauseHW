My Cause: Computer Science

My cause is a passion for computer science and a hope for more people to learn and explore computer science.

More people should learn how to code. In order to measure the current growth and widespreadness of computer science I looked at some data that shows counties in the united states and the amount of CS degrees awarded in that county

I think this is a good measure of how widespread computer science is and where there are areas that should increase computer science learning.
